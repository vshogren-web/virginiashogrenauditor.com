About Virginia

As a former U.S. Patent and Trademark Office Attorney, law firm associate, and principal of her own practice, Virginia has deep experience in detailed legal work, document accuracy, and regulatory compliance.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts and Science Degree with Distinction from Stanford University and a Juris Doctor Degree from the University of Washington School of Law.

Virginia is running for Clallam County Auditor to restore trust in our elections and to make sure your tax dollars are spent wisely.

My Platform

  • Election Integrity: Secure, transparent elections with paper ballots that voters can watch and count. Every ballot must be verifiable with full public oversight.
  • Fiscal Responsibility: Watch every county expense closely, cut waste, and run services efficiently so more money stays in your pocket.
  • Honest Government: Simple, accountable government that works for the people — not special interests. Real transparency and oversight.
